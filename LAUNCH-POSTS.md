## English

**Title:** Show HN: StockGuesser – guess the mystery stock of the day from attribute clues

I made **StockGuesser** 📈 — Wordle meets GeoGuessr, for the stock market.

Everyone gets the same mystery stock each day (UTC) and 6 guesses. Every guess gives
feedback across five attributes — sector, industry, country, market cap (with
higher/lower arrows), and shared index membership — and you end with a spoiler-free
emoji grid to share (🟩🟨⬜⬆️).

Beyond the daily there are 4 practice modes: Sporcle-style index recall (can you name
the whole S&P 500? The DAX? Nikkei 225?), a higher/lower market-cap battle, pin-the-HQ
on a world map, and guessing companies from naked 5-year price charts. You can even
build a quiz from your own watchlist.

Free, no ads, no signup, no tracking — stats live in your browser. ~4,400 companies
across 15 major indices, data auto-refreshes weekly. Fully static site, open source.

🎮 https://benginn.github.io/stockguesser/ · code: https://github.com/benginN/stockguesser

Feedback welcome — especially "I typed X and it wasn't accepted", those are one-line fixes.
