Privacy

Stock Guesser collects nothing.

  • No accounts, no cookies, no analytics, no trackers, no ads.
  • Your stats and streaks live only in your browser's localStorage and never leave your device. Clearing site data erases them.
  • The game makes no network requests except loading its own static files.
  • The site is hosted on GitHub Pages; GitHub may log requests (IP, user agent) to operate the service — see GitHub's privacy statement.

Market data is a weekly static snapshot compiled from public sources. Nothing here is investment advice. Questions: open an issue.
